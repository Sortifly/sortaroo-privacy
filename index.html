Privacy Policy for Sortaroo

Effective date: August 20, 2025

Overview. Sortaroo is a desktop app for MacOS and Windows PCs, that organizes media on your computer.

Data collection. Sortaroo does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. The app runs entirely on your computer and processes files locally.

How it works. You choose the source and destination folders. Sortaroo organizes media into date-based folders and can move exact duplicates to a Duplicates area. No analytics, no tracking, no ads.

Permissions. Sortaroo only accesses folders you select in the app. It does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

Children’s privacy. Sortaroo is not directed to children.

Changes. If our practices change, we’ll update this page.

Contact. Questions? info@sortaroo.com
